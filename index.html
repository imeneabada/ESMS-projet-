FAQ

1.what is retro reborn hackathon ?

Retro Reborn Hackathon is a three-day creative programming event for developers, designers, and AI enthusiasts, focused on reinventing classic 90s games using modern technology.

2.What is the composition of teams in Retro reborn hackathon ?

Each team combines the skills of developers, designers, and AI innovators, working hand in hand to breathe new life into vintage titles. Developers focus on game mechanics, responsiveness, and architecture; designers craft nostalgic yet modern interfaces; while AI experts integrate intelligent behaviors from adaptive enemies and smart NPCs to procedural level generation.

3.Are there workshops and mentoring ?

Yes. Technical workshops and expert mentoring sessions are provided to support teams throughout the hackathon.

4.How does the competition work ?

The competition runs over three days: team formation, prototype development, workshops, mentor feedback, and final presentations to the jury, concluding with an awards ceremony.

5.who can participate in retro reborn hackathon ?

Participation is open to anyone interested in programming, game design, development, or AI, regardless of their level of experience.

6.how do i participate in retro reborn hackathon

You can register directly through the **form available on this website**, and participants will receive all the necessary details for the hackathon.
